Shipping Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Shipping Origin

All orders are shipped from China.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation. You will receive a shipping confirmation with tracking information once your order has been dispatched.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Time

  • Standard International Shipping: 7–15 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 5–10 business days (available for select destinations, additional fees apply)

Shipping Rates

We offer simple, transparent shipping rates:

  • Standard Shipping: $6.99 per order
  • Free Shipping: On all orders totaling $49 or more

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout. The free shipping threshold applies to the subtotal before any applicable taxes.

Customs & Import Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, or other fees imposed by the destination country. These charges are the sole responsibility of the customer. Raindrop Crystal is not responsible for any customs-related delays or charges.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. Track your package using the carrier's official website.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost in transit or arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and arrange a replacement or refund as appropriate.

Contact

For shipping-related inquiries, please visit our Contact Us page.
