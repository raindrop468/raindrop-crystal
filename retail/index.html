Our Story · 私たちの物語

Every Crystal Has a Story to Tell

Raindrop was born from a rescued dog, a broken collar, and a belief that our pets deserve something as beautiful as the love they give us.
一匹の保護犬と、一つの壊れた首輪から生まれたブランド

01
🐾

It Started With Luna

Luna was a timid rescue dog who had been through three homes before she found her forever family. The day she was adopted, her new owner wanted to give her something special — a collar that said "You're home now. You're safe. You're loved." But every collar in the pet store looked the same. Nothing felt meaningful enough for that moment.

02
💎

Why Natural Crystals?

Crystals are nature's artwork — each one millions of years old, each one completely unique. Just like our pets. We chose natural crystals because no two are ever alike — just like the one-of-a-kind personality of every dog and cat. A crystal collar isn't just an accessory; it's a reflection of the individuality your pet brings to your life.

03
🕯️

A Timeless Keepsake

When a beloved pet crosses the rainbow bridge, we search for something to hold onto. Crystals, formed over millennia, carry a sense of permanence that flowers and photographs cannot match. A Raindrop crystal collar becomes a tangible memory — a beautiful reminder of the walks you shared, the quiet mornings, and the unconditional love. Many of our customers keep their pet's collar as a memorial piece long after their companion has passed.

04
💧

Why the Name "Raindrop"?

A single raindrop seems small and fleeting — yet it nourishes the earth, fills the rivers, and shapes entire landscapes over time. That's how we see the love between pets and their humans. It's built from thousands of tiny moments: a wagging tail at the door, a purr on the couch, a walk in the rain. Raindrop is a tribute to those small, ordinary moments that, together, create something extraordinary.

"We don't just make collars. We make keepsakes that celebrate the most honest love you'll ever know — the love of a pet."

— The Raindrop Team
