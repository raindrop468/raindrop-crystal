Our Story

Custom Pet Gifts Made With Care, Patience and Real Love for Pets

Raindrop Pet is a small custom pet gift studio focused on personalized pet portraits, photo keepsakes, memorial gifts and handmade pet accessories for dogs, cats and the families who love them.

Why We Started

Pets are not just animals in the home. They are family members, daily companions and, for many people, a source of comfort. We started Raindrop Pet because a custom gift made from a real pet photo can hold emotion in a way ordinary accessories cannot.

Our work is built around careful communication: understanding the pet, checking the photo, confirming details, and making each item feel personal rather than mass produced.

Where We Make Your Orders

Our custom orders are coordinated from China with a small production network of makers and artists experienced in pet photo gifts, crystal accessories and keepsake products. Each custom item is prepared after your photo and order details are confirmed.

What We Care About

Clear Communication

We help confirm photos, sizes and custom details before production.

Meaningful Gifts

Our products are designed for pet parents, memorial moments and personal keepsakes.

Careful Making

Each custom order is made individually, packed with care and shipped with tracking.
