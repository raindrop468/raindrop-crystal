Refund & Return Policy

Your satisfaction is important to us.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or you receive the wrong item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery.

Please include:

  • Your order number
  • Photos of the item
  • Photos of the packaging

We will gladly provide a replacement or full refund.

Personalized Products

Because personalized and custom-made products are created specifically for each customer, they cannot be returned or refunded unless:

  • The item arrives damaged
  • The item contains an error made by us
  • The wrong item was shipped

Non-Personalized Products

For non-customized items, returns may be accepted within 14 days of delivery.

Items must be unused and in their original condition.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

Lost Packages

If your package is lost during transit, please contact us. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and provide an appropriate solution.
