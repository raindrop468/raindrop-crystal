30-Day Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Our Guarantee

We take pride in the quality of our handcrafted crystal pet accessories. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day return and refund policy from the date of delivery.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return and refund, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • The item is in its original, unused condition with all tags and packaging intact.
  • The return request is initiated within 30 days of delivery.
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is provided.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or refunded:

  • Custom or personalized orders (made-to-measure sizing, custom engraving, etc.)
  • Items showing signs of wear, damage caused by the customer, or pet damage.
  • Sale / clearance items (marked as final sale).

Return Process

  1. Contact us at raindrop468@outlook.com or via WhatsApp with your order number and reason for return.
  2. We will provide return shipping instructions and the return address.
  3. Ship the item back within 14 days of receiving return instructions.
  4. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5–10 business days.

Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a manufacturing defect or an error on our part (e.g., wrong item shipped). In such cases, we will cover the return shipping cost.

Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at checkout (PayPal, credit card, etc.). Processing time may vary depending on your payment provider — typically 5–10 business days after refund approval.

Exchanges

If you need a different size or style, please initiate a return and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures the fastest processing time for your replacement.

Defective or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective item or an incorrect product, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the issue. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund including any shipping costs.

Contact

For all return and refund inquiries, please visit our Contact Us page.
